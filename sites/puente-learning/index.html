Our Programs

Everything we offer is led by credentialed, bilingual staff.

After-School Tutoring (K-8)

Monday through Thursday, 3:30-6pm. Reading, math, and writing instruction in English and Spanish. Small groups, credentialed bilingual tutors.

Reading & Math Intervention

Targeted support for students performing below grade level. Structured programs that close gaps without making kids feel behind.

Summer Enrichment Camps

Six-week summer program combining academic reinforcement with hands-on STEM projects. Keeps skills sharp without feeling like school.

Parent Workshops

Monthly workshops helping parents navigate the school system, understand report cards, and advocate for their children. In Spanish and English.

Our Story

Puente was founded by Carmen Delgado, a former Orange County Public Schools teacher who saw too many bilingual kids falling through the cracks.

We work with families in Conway, Azalea Park, and Meadow Woods. Our tutors are bilingual, credentialed, and from the community.

Carmen Delgado Founder & Director
