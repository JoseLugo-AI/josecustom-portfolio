About Masa Madre

Masa Madre started in 2019 out of a home kitchen on Curry Ford Road. What began as sourdough loaves sold at the Audubon Park Market grew into a full bakehouse serving East Orlando.

Everything is mixed, shaped, and baked on-site every morning. We source flour from Florida mills when we can and never use preservatives. If it's on the shelf, it was made today.

Over the years, what started as a weekend hobby has grown into a kitchen that feeds hundreds of families each week. We honor the process -- slow fermentation, real butter, no shortcuts.
