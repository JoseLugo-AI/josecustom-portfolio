Who we are

Southpaw opened in College Park in 2020 with 12 members and a rented warehouse. Coach Andre Watts -- former Army combatives instructor, brown belt in BJJ -- built the gym on one principle: train hard, leave your ego at the door. We're at 150 members now across all programs.
