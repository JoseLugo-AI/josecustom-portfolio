01 Services

We handle the financial complexity so Orlando's small business owners and freelancers can focus on what they do best. From annual tax filing to year-round bookkeeping, every engagement starts with understanding your business first.

Tax Preparation

Individual and business returns. We handle everything from simple W-2s to multi-entity business filings. E-file included, always. We stay current on Florida-specific deductions and federal changes so you don't have to.

Bookkeeping

Monthly reconciliation, P&L statements, and balance sheets. You run your business. We keep the books clean, accurate, and ready when you need them. No surprises at year-end.

Entity Formation

LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp. We'll help you pick the right structure and file the paperwork. Includes first-year tax planning so your entity works for you from day one.

IRS Representation

Got a letter from the IRS? Don't panic. We handle audits, payment plans, and penalty abatement on your behalf. Most issues have straightforward solutions.

Sample Client Scenarios
Freelancer outgrowing spreadsheets

You've been tracking income in a spreadsheet and doing your own taxes. It worked when you had 3 clients. Now you have 15 and you missed a quarterly estimate. We take over the books, set up proper tracking, and file quarterly so you never get behind.

04 AI Integration

We use AI to eliminate the tedious parts of tax preparation and bookkeeping, so our team spends more time advising and less time on data entry. Every tool we use is secure, compliant, and reviewed by a human before anything goes out.

Technology should make your accountant faster, not replace them. That's the line we hold.

How AI Works at Aldea
Document Intake

Clients upload tax documents to our encrypted portal. AI extracts key data, pre-fills worksheets, and flags missing documents before the first meeting. Less back-and-forth, faster turnaround.

Categorization

Bank transactions are automatically categorized and matched to the correct accounts. Our team reviews and corrects exceptions. The AI learns your business over time.

Anomaly Detection

Unusual charges, duplicate transactions, and potential audit flags are surfaced automatically. We catch problems months before tax season, not during it.
